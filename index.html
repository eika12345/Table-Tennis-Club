Welcome to Clissold Table Tennis Club

Join a community of passionate players. Train, compete, and grow your game with us.

About us

We are a friendly, community-driven club based in the heart of North London. Whether you’re picking up a paddle for the first time or you’ve been playing for years, our doors are open to everyone who loves the game.

We run regular coaching sessions, open practice nights, and league competitions for all skill levels. Our qualified coaches are passionate about helping players improve their technique, footwork, and match strategy in a supportive environment.

Beyond training, the club is all about community — connecting people of all ages through sport, teamwork, and fun. Come for the table tennis, stay for the camaraderie!

Find Us

Clissold Table Tennis Club is based at Clissold Leisure Centre in Stoke Newington, North London. We're easily accessible by public transport and have plenty of local parking nearby.
